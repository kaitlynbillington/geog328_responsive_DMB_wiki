Notable Awards

Throughout their career, Dave Matthews Band has received nominations and awards from various prestigious organizations notably the Grammy Awards and Rock and Roll Hall of Fame! Here are some of their awards and nominations over the years:

Grammy Awards

  • 1996: Nominated for Best Rock Vocal Performance by a Duo or Group for "What Would You Say"
  • 1996: Nominated for Best Music Video, Short Form for "What Would You Say"
  • 1997: Winner of Best Rock Vocal Performance by a Duo or Group for "So Much to Say"
  • 1997: Nominated for Best Rock Album for "Crash"
  • 1997: Nominated for Best Rock Song for "Too Much"
  • 1998: Nominated for Best Rock Song for "Crash Into Me"
  • 1998: Nominated for Best Rock Vocal Performance by a Duo or Group for "Crash Into Me"
  • 1999: Nominated for Best Rock Album for "Before These Crowded Streets"
  • 1999: Nominated for Best Pop Performancce by a Duo or Group with Vocal for "Crush"
  • 2000: Nominated for Best Pop Collaboration with Vocals for "Love of My Life" with Santana
  • 2002: Nominated for Best Rock Vocal Performance by a Duo or Group for "The Space Between"
  • 2003: Nominated for Best Pop Performance by a Duo or Group with Vocal for "Where Are You Going"
  • 2010: Nominated for Best Rock Album and Album or the Year for "Big Whisky and the GrooGrux King"

Rock and Roll Hall of Fame

  • 2019: Nominated for Induction into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame
  • 2024: Inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame
