Members of Dave Matthews Band

Over the years, Dave Matthews Band has played with a variety of players, both live and in studio. While the band has featured many instruments and players, they have maintained a core group of full-time members since their official formation in 1991. Below is a table outlining these members.

Name Instrument Years Active
Dave Matthews Lead Vocals, Acoustic Guitar 1991-Present
Carter Beauford Drums 1991-Present
LeRoi Moore Saxophone, Flute 1991-2008
Stefan Lessard Bass Guitar 1991-Present
Peter Greisar Keyboard 1991-1993
Boyd Tinsley Violin 1991-2018
Tim Reynolds Electric Guitar 1998-Present
Rashawn Ross Trumpet 2005-Present
Jeff Coffin Saxophone, Flute 2008-Present
Buddy Strong Keyboard 2018-Present
